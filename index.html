    Mountain Hugh's Construction

    This two player game was created in less than 48 hours for the Global Game Jam 2018. Pit yourself against a friend in this battle of wits and I-beams. Built from the ground up, all of the code, sounds and art assets were generated during the jam. It's fun mechanics and comedic presentation won it the "most fun" title!

    "Whale's Wail" Alarm System

    Using arduino in combination with external sensors and wiring, our team created a motion sensor that would emit a high pitched sound if triggered. With password setup on startup and customizable alarm sounds and tunes, programming and wiring this alarm was an insightful experience.
